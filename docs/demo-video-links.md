The videos demonstrate the module behavior and end-to-end flows. Some terminal
segments were recorded before Logos announced the final LEZ `v0.2.0` tag, so the
current hosted-testnet transaction hashes in
`docs/testnet-v020-final-evidence-20260701.md` and
`docs/prize-submission-dossier.md` are the source of truth for the final
submission. The final evidence refresh uses `RISC0_DEV_MODE=0` and includes
hosted `wallet.send`, `agent.task` payment, `program.deploy`, and
`program.call` hashes.

The videos are split to match `docs/final-demo-recording-guide.md`.
